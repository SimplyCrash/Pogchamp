What We Offer

Unique experience

Blending the escape room concept with a full 3D cinematic experience to deliver a unique, immersive and mind-blowing adventure.

Thrill

The thrills appeal to newcomers or veterans of VR or escape rooms. This is multisensory VR with wind and heat on your face. The smell of gunpowder and smoke lingering in the air. Physical vibrations making the action FEEL REAL!

Fun with Friends

Played in groups of 2 to 5 players where each player has their own dedicated room – you will talk to each other, walk around, bend, throw objects and collaborate together to solve the puzzles and challenges.

Modern technology

Escape Virtual is one of the world’s best team-based virtual reality experiences with its new advanced technology.

How Does it work?

Arrive

Arrive half an hour before

Simply arrive half an hour before your booking time. We will get the team together and ready for the briefing. 

Briefing

Briefing at Quarter Past

During the briefing we will tell you everything you need to know about our escape rooms, how they work and all about your chosen room.

Crack Clues

Start on the hour

This is where the fun starts! You will have 50 minutes to work with your team to crack clues, solve puzzles and escape.
